ABOUT US

Behind Sinergi Medik

Incorporated in 2019, Sinergi Medik is a professional distributor of orthopedic trauma implants and consumables based in Pahang, Malaysia. We deliver the best medical solutions through a strategic partnership with the well-known Mondeal Medical System GmbH. Led by our three founders, Mr. Logan Thangamani, Mr. Vivek Naidu and Mr. Devasharma Gangadaran, we aim to provide trusted innovative medical solutions to customers across Malaysia in hopes of continuing growth in the local medical market

We believe that our success depends on our core assets. By leveraging on a professional team and strategic business associates, Sinergi Medik is certain to empower effective marketing and excellence to customers across the country
